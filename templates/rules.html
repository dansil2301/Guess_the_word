Rules

  • You have to guess the word letter by letter
  • Every guessed letter will give you 100 points
  • Letters guessed in row will give extra points
    extra points = 10 * (words in a row - 1)
  • Every guessed word will give you 500 points
  • You have 5 tries to guess a word otherwise you lose
    every guessed letter gives you extra try
  • The default difficulty is normal, you can change it
